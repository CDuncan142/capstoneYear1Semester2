Fundraisers

Our newest project in the works 'it always makes a difference' takes on a more daunting goal than our previous fundraisers. Though our goal as a non-profit is to make a difference in Arizona Schools, we've decided to extend our reach. In this new project, we plan to inspire young minds all over the world. Though reaching even a small area is extremely resource intensive, 'it always makes a difference' is the philosophy we go by.

Books are something we tend to accumalate over the years, even to the point that we forget how many we have. 'More than a book' is our initiative to 'rescue' unused books and use them to inspire a love of reading in the younger generation
