The pencil is mightier than the sword

Graphite is a non-profit based mainly in Arizona. Our goal is to offset the unfair financial circumstances of many arizona schools through the use of financial aid and other services. Additionally we hold multiple fundraisers throughout the year to raise the funds that allow this to happen.

Schools are undersupplied in unique and various ways that can't be solved by money alone. Graphite creates unique solutions to these problems that range from individually tailored fundraisers to handmaking models or learning tools help. Funds often go towards things that benifit the students learning experience rather than the school directly. This can often allow the higher test scores needed to increase funding from the state. students understand a complex lesson.

Our newest Fundraiser

Our newest project in the works 'it always makes a difference' takes on a more daunting goal than our previous fundraisers. Though our goal as a non-profit is to make a difference in Arizona Schools, we've decided to extend our reach. In this new project, we plan to inspire young minds all over the world. Though reaching even a small area is extremely resource intensive, 'it always makes a difference' is the philosophy we go by.


If we didn't ask ourselves why we do what we do we wouldn't be a good non-profit, and we certainly aren't writting this here for our sake. The truth of the matter is Arizona schools are hurting and unless we do something about it then the futures of their students will reflect that.
